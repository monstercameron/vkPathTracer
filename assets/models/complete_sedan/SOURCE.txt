Complete Sedan model source

Source repository: https://github.com/ChenZongHeng/car
Source asset: data/benchi2.glb
Source commit used: 0ff70a3bd8d2b4970cb32470eba2f7ae4a2f137a
Repository license: MIT

The original GLB is stored as complete_sedan.glb. complete_sedan.obj and
complete_sedan.mtl were generated from that GLB for vkPathTracer's OBJ scene
asset loader. The conversion normalizes the car to a 4.8 unit footprint, places
the bottom of the model on Y=0, and remaps the GLB material buckets into
path-tracer material families for car paint, tinted glass, rubber, chrome,
brushed aluminum, plastic trim, lamp lenses, and interior fabric.
