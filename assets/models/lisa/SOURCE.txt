Lisa scene model asset

Model: Suzanne / Blender monkey head
Downloaded from:
https://github.com/alecjacobson/common-3d-test-models/blob/master/data/suzanne.obj

The source repository lists the original source as Blender. The OBJ has been
normalized for this scene so that Blender Y maps to vkPathTracer world Y and
Blender Z points toward the authored camera.
