Local conversion notes:
- The original glTF mesh was converted to OBJ/MTL for the current scene asset
  expander, which instantiates OBJ scene assets.
- Vertex positions include the authored glTF node transforms and were normalized
  to a 4-unit horizontal footprint with the lowest point on Y=0.
- The MTL uses vkPathTracer material hints such as family, clearcoat,
  transmission, and sheen so the renderer can preserve the car paint, glass,
  metal, and fabric intent even before full texture sampling is available.
