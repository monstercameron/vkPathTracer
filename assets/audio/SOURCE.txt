Audio Lua Interaction Demo sources
==================================

All files in this folder are real recorded audio assets used by
assets/scenes/audio_lua_interaction_demo.json.

Modification notes: files are kept as downloaded MP3 source clips with no
semantic edits. Engine playback may apply runtime gain, pitch, looping, and
spatialization.

Allowed usage: prototype/demo playback in this repository and derived builds,
subject to the public-domain/CC0 source licenses listed below.
